Poker simulator

This application is a simulator to test your ability to choose the right decision when you are in small blind with a short stack (less than 20bb) in a heads-up poker game.

The expected result is the Nash Equilibrium of the situation, which means that your opponant plays according to the Nash Equilibrium.

The data used to build this application comes from the HeadsUp Push/Fold Nash Equilibrium chart of the Holdemresources website.
